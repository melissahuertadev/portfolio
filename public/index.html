Workflow

Aware

Hellooo! The first step is to know you and your current situation, to be aware of your or your business's problems and needs, so together we can look for the best solution.

Land

Once we have defined the problem or need, I'll start brainstorming possible solutions and of course if you have already something in mind, that's beyond awesome! So we can finally land the idea that will be turned into app.

Prototype

Great! Now we have our idea landed. I'll share with you some wireframes and after we choose the one that suits you best, it's time for me to show you high fidelity prototype of the app.
